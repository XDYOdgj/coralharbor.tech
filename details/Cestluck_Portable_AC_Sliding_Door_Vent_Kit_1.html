Cestluck Portable AC Sliding Door Vent Kit

Keep your room cool and your AC running efficiently with the Cestluck Portable AC Sliding Door Vent Kit. This compact and reliable solution is designed to fit a range of window sizes, from 17" to 95", making it the perfect companion for any portable air conditioner setup.

Whether your sliding window is vertical or horizontal, this vent kit ensures a secure fit that minimizes air leaks and maximizes airflow. The built-in bird guard and high-quality sealing tape work together to prevent debris from entering the exhaust hose, keeping your indoor space clean and comfortable.

With no complicated tools required, installation is quick and hassle-free. Simply adjust the number of plates from 1 to 6 to match your window dimensions and install the kit. It's compatible with multiple AC brands including LG, Whynter, and Haier, giving you greater flexibility in your cooling options.

Enhance your home's cooling efficiency and enjoy a more pleasant living environment. The Cestluck AC Sliding Door Vent Kit is an excellent choice for anyone seeking a practical, easy-to-use solution that improves both performance and comfort.
